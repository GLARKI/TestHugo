Moon is a radiologist and research software engineer working on a medical AI research platform and on optimization of medical data annotation. He is currently also working on the research of imaging biomarkers derived from PSMA PET. The goal is to develop fully automated algorithms for imaging biomarkers that can be applied in the clinical routine, e.g., to assess therapy response or biochemical recurrence in prostate cancer.
